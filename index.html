I am a PhD student with Dr. Mukesh Kumar at the University of Alabama. Here, I am working at the "CENTER FOR COMPLEX HYDROSYSTEMS RESEARCH" laboratory (Dept. of Civil, Construction, and Environmental Engineering). The overarching goal of my research is to improve the understanding and prediction of ecohydrological processes through synergistic use of data analytics and modeling.
